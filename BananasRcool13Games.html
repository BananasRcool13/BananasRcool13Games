About Me

Hi, I'm Jackson Wright (aka BananasRcool13). I'm a game developer and basketball enthusiast. My dream is to one day make it to the NBA, and I am passionate about creating games that are exciting and engaging.

When I'm not working on my games, I'm either playing basketball or dreaming up new ideas for future projects. I’m inspired by my favorite NBA player, Dyson Daniels, and strive to channel that determination into everything I do.
